White Bread

White Bread

Ingredients

  • 11 c. flour
  • 2 T. yeast
  • 6 T. sugar
  • 6 T. oil
  • 4T salt
  • 1 c. milk
  • 3 c. water
  • Equipment: kettle, large mixing bowl, large mixing spoon (wooden), 4 cup glass measuring cup, plastic wrap, and dishes.

Steps

  1. Put water in kettle adn turn heat up.
  2. Put 4 cups of flour and all other dry ingredients into a very large bowl and stir to combine.
  3. Pour oil and milk into large glass measuring cup. If you don't have a large measuring cup, combine ingredients in a large bowl.
  4. Start adding the 3 cups of hot water. When you only have about half a cup left, feel the water and keep stirring.
  5. Mix the liquids into the flour with a large spoon, i use a wooden spoon.
  6. Pour remaining flour into the bowl, stirring as you go along. When it is to hard, add flour to you hands, then use your hadns to finish the mixing.
  7. Stop adding flour or add more flour until it reaches the right consistency where it is no longer sticking to your hands.
  8. Knead about ten minutes. You can't overknead when you are doing it by hand.
  9. Stop kneading when it is satiny and it springs back right away when you press on it.
  10. Pour oil arouind the bowl. Spin the dough around in the oil to cover the dough and bowl with oil.
  11. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and then a towel.
  12. If you are in a hurry put it in a warm place.
  13. After it has doubled in size, punch it down and form it into the shape you would like to cook it.
  14. Let it rise another 40 minutes before baking at 400 degrees in the oven for 20 minutes.

Red Lentil Soups

Red Lentil Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 carrots
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 1/2 c. red lentils
  • 1 T. Vegeta
  • salt and pepper as desired
  • Equipment: Soup pot, stirring spoon, sharp knife, grater.

Steps

  1. Grate two carrots.
  2. Finely dice a medium onion.
  3. Saute the onion in a splotch of oil in the pot. Traditionally you would add a tablespoon or two of tomato paste after sauting the onions.
  4. Add 7 cups of water to the carrots, and lentils.
  5. Season with vegata.
  6. Bring to a boil and the turn to the lowest simmer.
  7. It will take about an hour for the lentils to cook.

Notes: You can let it cook for as long as you would like, but keep it on a very low heat and stir whenever you walk past.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 3 1/4 c Flour
  • 2/3 c Sugar
  • 1 c Brown sugar
  • 1 t Baking Powder
  • 1/4 t Baking Soda
  • 1/2 t Salt
  • 1 t Cinnamon
  • 1/4 t Nutmeg
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 1/4 c shortening or butter
  • Your desired amount of Chocolate Chips.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Then you will mix together, Flour, Baking Powder, Baking Soda, Salt, Cinnamon, and Nutmeg.
  3. Then mix in a seperate bowl, Sugar, Brown Sugar, Eggs, and Butter/shortening.
  4. Then put both mixtures in the same bowl and stir well.
  5. Add your desired amount of chocolate chips.
  6. Roll into balls and place them onto a baking sheet.
  7. Cook them in the oven for about 10-12 minutes.

Note: You can also place your cookie dough into the fridge to set better.

Strawberry Smoothie

Stawberry Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of frozen strawberries
  • 1 banana (room temperature)
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup milk (or almond milk or oat milk)
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons maple syrup, honey, or agave syrup
  • 1/2 cup ice
  • Optional add-ins: 1 tablespoon almond butter, 1/4 teaspoons vanilla, fresh mint leaves or basil leaves

    • Steps

    1. Place all ingredients in a blender.
    2. Break the banana into pieces.
    3. Blend until creamy and frothy, scraping down the sides if necessary.
    4. If desired, garnish with a frozen strawberry and mint sprig.
    5. serve immediately or store in a covered jar in the refrigerator for 2 days, max.

    Note: For vegan: blend 2 cups frozen strawberries, 2 bananas (room temperature), 1/2 cup water and 8 ice cubes.

    Spaghetti

    Spaghetti

    Ingredients

    • 1 pound of Hamburger
    • 2 cubes of beef bouillion
    • 1 can of tomato sauce 8 oz
    • 1 can of tomato paste 6 oz
    • pepper to taste
    • 2 cups of hot water
    • 2 teaspoons sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoons of dried basil
    • 1/2 dried oregano
    • dash of garlic
    • 16 oz spaghetti noodles

    Steps

    1. Brown hamburger in a large pan.
    2. Once cooked, throw in salt, pepper,tomato sauce and paste, water(with bouillion cubes in it), sugar, basil, oregano and garlic. Simmer on low for an hour.
    3. A few minutes before the hour is done, cook box of spaghetti noodles as directed on the package.
    4. Once the noodles are cooked, drain and add to Spaghetti sauce. ENJOY!
